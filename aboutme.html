My name is Clarissa Alberts and I work full-time as a freelance digital artist. I’m South African but currently living in Uruguay.

My ambition is to work in the video game industry as a character concept artist and to bring my ideas and creativity to life. My ideal work environment would be where I can constantly be collaborating and communicating with like-minded artists. I thrive off of being able to give and receive critique from fellow artists and being able to grow and improve alongside them.

For any inquiries, you can reach me via my contact page.

My resume is available upon request.

I can also be found on Instagram and Twitter!
